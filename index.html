📖 Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Open the menu (☰) and go to DNS. Choose one and click on it and then click on allow (Recommended: Vũ Văn Khơi DNS).
  2. After that, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management. Click on the DNS you just installed (example: KhơinDNS), then tap Install. Enter your device passcode, tap Done, then tap Install and Install again.
  3. Go to J•SIGN and try each one until one installs successfully. If one of them has a 🟢, try that one first.
  4. Once installed, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management.
  5. Click on the installed J•SIGN profile and tap Trust and Trust again.
  6. Return to this website, go to CERTIFICATE, and click on the one that matches your E-SIGN and click on the blue download button.
  7. In J•SIGN, click on Ok then click on agree and agree again, tap the 3 dots → Import → click on the certificate you just installed.
  8. Tap the certificate again, then Unzip, open the folder, and find the file ending in .p12.
  9. Click on Import Certificate Management and enter: AppleP12.com.
  10. Download the IPA you want and then go in J•SIGN, click the 3 dots → Import → click on the IPA you just installed and click import
  11. After that in J•SIGN click on Apps, click on the icon of the IPA you just imported and click signature then click signature again and wait it loads then click on Install and Install again.
